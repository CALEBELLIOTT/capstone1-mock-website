Our Promise To You

We will protect you from all of the elk in the parks

That's right. The elk in the park account for 95% of deaths dating back to 1995. On average, there are 20,000 deaths yearly from these dangerous predators. Humans at one point made up 30% of these animal's diets. We are proud to announce that as of last year, these deaths plummeted to a mere 27%. The park is continuing to take action against this invasive and dangerous species.

We have spreadsheets and statistics on all of the deaths from 1995 to present in addition to recommended precautionary measures for those entering the parks.
